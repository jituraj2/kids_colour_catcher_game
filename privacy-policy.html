Privacy Policy

At Color Catcher Game, your child's privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we do and do not collect when someone uses our game.

👶 No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Our game does not ask for names, email addresses, location data, or any other personal details.

📵 No Ads, No Trackers

The game does not contain third-party ads, tracking scripts, analytics tools, or cookies. Kids can play freely without being exposed to any marketing or third-party content.

🎮 Game Permissions

The only browser permission used is for audio playback (speech instructions and background music). This helps guide children through the game using spoken color prompts. No microphone or camera access is requested or used.

💻 Local Device Only

All game activity happens entirely in the browser. No data is sent to any server. Scores, settings, and gameplay are not stored or transmitted.

👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents

This game was created to be a fun, educational tool for young learners. We value a safe and private digital environment for children. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at your@email.com.
